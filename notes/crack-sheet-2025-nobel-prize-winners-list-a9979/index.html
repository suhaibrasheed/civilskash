Crack Sheet: 2025 Nobel Prize Winners List

Maria Corina Machado won the Peace Prize for her struggle to restore democracy in Venezuela.
László Krasznahorkai received the Literature Prize for his visionary, art-affirming writing.
The Medicine Prize went to Mary E. Brunkow, Fred Ramsdell, and Shimon Sakaguchi for uncovering mechanisms of immune tolerance and the role of regulatory T cells.
The Physics Prize honored John Clarke, Michel Devoret, and John Martinis for breakthroughs in quantum phenomena in electric circuits and chips.
The Chemistry Prize went to Susumu Kitagawa, Richard Robson, and Omar Yaghi for pioneering MOFs and advancing their use in carbon capture and water harvesting.
The Economics Prize recognized Joel Mokyr, Philippe Aghion, and Peter Howitt for explaining how innovation and creative destruction drive long-term economic growth.
