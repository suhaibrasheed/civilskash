Schedules of Indian Constitution Mnemonic

Memorizing the 12 Schedules of the Indian Constitution is crucial for matching questions in Prelims. Use the famous trick: 'TEARS OF OLD PM'. T stands for Territories (1st Schedule), E for Emoluments (2nd), A for Affirmations (3rd), and R for Rajya Sabha seats (4th). The 10th Schedule, added by the 52nd Amendment Act of 1985, deals with the Anti-Defection Law, often referred to as the 'Dal Badal' law.
