Turkey make world's first Drone that can kill Rafale

Bayraktar KIZILELMA became the first unmanned combat aircraft in the world to destroy a jet-powered aerial target using a radar-guided beyond-visual-range missile.
During a live test off the coast of Sinop (Black Sea) , KIZILELMA used MURAD AESA radar (by Aselsan) to detect and track the target, then fired a domestically-developed Gökdoğan missile (by TÜBİTAK SAGE), scoring a direct hit — demonstrating a complete indigenous radar-to-missile kill chain.
This historic event marks a paradigm shift in aerial warfare, proving that UCAVs can now undertake air-to-air combat and Turkish Kizilelma has ability to take down sophisticated Aircrafts like Rafale.
