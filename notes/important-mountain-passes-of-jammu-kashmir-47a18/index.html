Important Mountain Passes of Jammu Kashmir

Map-based questions often ask to arrange passes from North to South. The Banihal Pass connects Jammu to Srinagar and houses the Jawahar Tunnel. The Zoji La pass connects Srinagar to Leh and is vital for Ladakh's connectivity. The Burzil Pass historically connects the Kashmir Valley to the Deosai Plains of Gilgit. The Khardung La in Ladakh is one of the highest motorable roads in the world.
